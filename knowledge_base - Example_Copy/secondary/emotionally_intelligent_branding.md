# Emotionally Intelligent Branding

Emotionally intelligent branding understands the audience's inner state, not just their demographic profile.

## Core insights

- Machines can detect patterns, but humans are needed to interpret nuance, cultural sensitivity, and the deeper emotional job a brand is doing.
- Generic marketing copy says what the product is.
- Emotionally intelligent branding says what the product means in a person's life.
- Emotional resonance often depends on leaving room for the audience to complete the meaning themselves.

## Implications

- Build narratives around feeling before feature.
- Use subtext before explanation.
- Use symbol before slogan.
- Prioritize human truth before optimization.
